* ArcGIS Online Hosted Portals Only

Running this task will attempt to update your org's URL key. Your URL key is used to create your org's URL. The format is http://<urlKey>.maps.arcgis.com.

If the urlKey is successfully updated, all urls of Esri-hosted web mapping applications in your org will be updated. After the task is complete, you will need to update any reference to these URLs outside of ArcGIS Online.
